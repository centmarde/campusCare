About CampusCare
CampusCare is a mobile application designed to streamline the process of reporting and resolving issues within a university campus. Developed with the goal of enhancing campus safety and efficiency, CampusCare allows students, faculty, and staff to quickly report various types of problems, such as maintenance issues, safety concerns, and campus feedback.

Key Features
Problem Reporting: Users can easily report issues by submitting a description and attaching relevant photos.
Real-time Updates: Receive notifications on the status of reported problems and their resolutions.
Anonymous Reporting: Maintain privacy while reporting sensitive issues.
Feedback System: Provide feedback on campus facilities and services to improve overall experience.

CampusCare is committed to improving the campus experience for everyone and promoting a safe and conducive learning environment. Download CampusCare today and be part of the solution!
